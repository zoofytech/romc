Adventure Quest

Credits: [MOD] Rendang

Adventurer Rank Scout Quest

  1. Adventurer Level must be Lv5
  2. Talk to NPC at north side of Prontera to start the quest
  3. Go to Prontera West Field talk to Swordman NPC
  4. Take photo of Yoyo
  5. Talk again to Swordman NPC
  6. Beat 100 Rockers
  7. Talk again to Swordman NPC
  8. Talk to NPC at north side of Prontera to finish the quest

Adventurer Rank F Quest

  1. Adventurer Level must be Lv10
  2. Talk to Adventure NPC at north side of Prontera to start the quest
  3. Go to Izlude Town, talk to NPC
  4. Take photo of Whispers
  5. Beat 100 Pirate Skeleton
  6. Beat 50 Skeleton
  7. Talk to Adventure NPC at north side of Prontera to finish the quest

Adventurer Rank E Quest

  1. Adventurer Level must be Lv15
  2. Talk to Lord Knight NPC at Mt. Mjolnir
  3. Take photo of Ghostring
  4. Give NPC the following:
    • 5 Magic Wand Marin
    • 5 Camouflage Scroll
    • 2 Smokie Leaf
  5. Talk to NPC at Mt. Mjolnir
  6. Go back to Prontera talk to Adventure NPC to finish the quest

Adventurer Rank D Quest

  1. Adventurer Level must be Lv20
  2. Start quest at Prontera, and go to Morroc then talk to Hunter NPC
  3. Beat 50 Desert Wolf
  4. Give the Hunter NPC the following:
    • 1 Emperium
    • 3 Fabric
    • 3 Four Leaf Clover
    • 10 Dead Branch
  5. 1 Eva (doll in toy shop)
  6. Take photo and beat Osiris
  7. Talk to Hunter NPC
  8. Go to Adventure NPC at north side of Prontera to finish

Adventurer Rank C Quest

  1. Adventurer Level must be Lv25
  2. Have Certificate of tutor from Gadar Quest
  3. Start quest at Adventure NPC at north side of Prontera
  4. Go to Payon and talk to Sniper NPC
  5. Must have at least 1M zeny to show to him
  6. Follow the quest tracker
  7. Submit 1000 Evil Horns at Prontera NPC Near Church
  8. Beat Doppelganger and get his soul
  9. Clear Endless Tower level 70+
  10. Go back to Payon South Field and give NPC the following:
    • 1 Orc Nail
    • 1 Antenna
    • 1 Dragon Scale
    • 500 Necklace of Wisdom
  11. Go back to Payon Town talk to Hunter NPC to finish

Adventurer Rank B Quest

  1. Adventurer Level must be Lv30
  2. Quest starts at Payon, talk to Hunter NPC
  3. Give the NPC 1 Biotite
  4. Go to Prontera, give Adventure NPC 1 Pearl
  5. Go to Clock Tower 1F, talk to NPC, follow, then kill SinX
  6. Follow the quest tracker
  7. Go back to Payon, give NPC 1 Button of Time
  8. Talk again to finish
